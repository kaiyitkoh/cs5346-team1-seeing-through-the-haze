Want to explore the data yourself?

The datasets behind this project are publicly available. Download them and run your own analysis.

Air Quality (PSI & PM2.5)

24-hour PSI and hourly PM2.5 readings from Singapore's National Environment Agency (NEA). Covers all monitoring stations.

Coverage: 2014–2024 · Retrieved: Mar 2025

data.gov.sg ↗

Rainfall (NEA)

Historical daily rainfall totals from NEA monitoring stations across Singapore. Used to evaluate whether rain suppresses regional haze.

Coverage: 2014–2024 · Retrieved: Mar 2025

data.gov.sg ↗

Fire Detections (NASA FIRMS)

VIIRS satellite-detected fire hotspots from Suomi NPP and NOAA-20 at 375m resolution. Archive and near-real-time data for Southeast Asia.

Coverage: 2014–2024 · Retrieved: Mar 2025

firms.modaps.eosdis.nasa.gov ↗

Wind (ECMWF ERA5)

Hourly u/v wind components from ECMWF's ERA5 reanalysis at 0.25° resolution, accessed via the Copernicus Climate Data Store. Used to derive daily wind bearing and alignment with Riau and Johor fire sources.

Coverage: 2014–2024 · Retrieved: Mar 2025

cds.climate.copernicus.eu ↗

ENSO Index (ONI)

3-month running mean of sea surface temperature anomalies in the Niño 3.4 region, published by NOAA's Climate Prediction Center.

Coverage: Historical · Retrieved: Mar 2025

cpc.ncep.noaa.gov ↗
